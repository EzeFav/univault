Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

UniVault ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register and use UniVault:

• Full name and email address
• University, department and level
• Payment transaction references (we do not store card details)
• App usage data to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

• Create and manage your account
• Process payments for academic materials
• Send notifications about new content and account activity
• Provide personalised AI tutoring through ExamPass AI
• Improve our app and services

Payment Security

All payments are processed securely through Paystack. We do not store your card details. Payment references are stored to verify your purchases.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate UniVault (Firebase, Paystack, Google AI).

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at univaultapps@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at univaultapps@gmail.com or follow us on @UniVaultapps.
