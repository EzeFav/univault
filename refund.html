Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At SwiftLogix Solutions, we are committed to ensuring that UniVault provides immense value to your academic journey. Because UniVault offers immediate access to premium digital academic resources—including Past Questions, Notes, Handouts, and ExamPass AI—our refund policy is specifically tailored to the nature of digital goods.

1. Digital Subscriptions & In-App Purchases

Due to the immediate access granted to digital content upon purchase, all sales are generally considered final. We do not offer refunds for "buyer's remorse" or if you simply decide you no longer need the study materials after you have accessed or viewed them.

2. Exceptional Circumstances for Refunds

We understand that technical issues can occur. We may offer a refund at our sole discretion under the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If a technical fault on our end prevents you from accessing the premium features or documents you purchased, and our support team is unable to resolve the issue within 72 hours.
  • Duplicate Charges: If you were mistakenly billed multiple times for the exact same transaction.

3. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund under the exceptional circumstances listed above, please contact our support team within 7 days of the original transaction.

Please include your full name, the email address associated with your UniVault account, and your proof of purchase or Paystack transaction receipt in your request.
